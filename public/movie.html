The Best Movies According to Yashita

Here are a list of my top 3 favourite movies

Rang De Basanti

In terms of writing, direction and Acting I feel this movie has excelled. Apart this is very close to my heart, emotions portrayed are quite moving

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

This movie is quite distinct. I enjoyed the rollercoaster of scenes , numerous frame changes. Story is also very unique and makes the movie watch-worthy

Tumbbaad

This is a masterpiece. Excellent cinematography. Mindblowing and scary scenes. Awesome story
